Sprint 2 Core - Learning Plan

25 April 2019

Long term goals

I want to use the skills I and knowledge I learn into my existing career. I want to continually build on my software development skill set and find a new niche.

Commitment to the EDA Programme

My basic commitment will be to get sprint work done by COB Friday. I am fortunate to be in a position where I can put a lot of time into the course including weekend, though I will respect other team mates boundaries with their personal commitments, and encourage them to be realistic about these. I will make a concerted effort to not leaving my facilitators or team mates hanging.

I will seek help by keeping to my check-in and meeting times, and communicating apologies when I cannot attend.

I have made a commitment to devoting a significant amount of time to study. I will need to commit to looking after my personal fitness and well-being and attend my sports teams games when this does not interfere with study.

Over foundations I have also committed to attending to some minor pieces of work, and during boot camp I will also make myself available to answer urgent work calls during specific hours.

I expect myself to be direct and honest with how my study is going, and I expect honest feedback. If team members are having significant issues or causing major disharmony I expect the facilitation team to intervene in a sensitive and timely manner, if required.
